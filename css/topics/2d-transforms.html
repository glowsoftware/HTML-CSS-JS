2D Transforms

The translate() Method

The translate() method moves an element from its current position. This div element is moved 20 pixels to the right, and 20 pixels down from its current position.

The rotate() Method

The rotate() method rotates an element clockwise or counter-clockwise. This div element is rotated clockwise 20 degrees.

The scale() Method

This div element is two times of its original width, and three times of its original height.
This div element has 70% of its original width, and 70% of its original height.

The skewX() Method

This div element is skewed 10 degrees along the X-axis.

The skewY() Method

This div element is skewed 10 degrees along the Y-axis.

The skew() Method

This div element is skewed 10 degrees along the X-axis, and 5 degrees along the Y-axis.

The matrix() Method

The matrix() method combines all the 2D transform methods into one.

matrix(scaleX(),skewY(),skewX(),scaleY(),translateX(),translateY())

transform: matrix(1, 0.1, 0.1, 1, 50, 50);;
