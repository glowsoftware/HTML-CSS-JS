Use of the auto Value

You can set the margin property to auto to horizontally center the element within its container. The element will then take up the specified width, and the remaining space will be split equally between the left and right margins:

This div will be centered because it has margin: auto;

Margin Collapse

In this example the div1 has a bottom margin of 70px and the div2 has a top margin of 50px. Then, the vertical margin between div1 and div2 should have been 120px (70px + 50px). However, due to margin collapse, the actual margin ends up being 70px. This does not happen on left and right margins.
